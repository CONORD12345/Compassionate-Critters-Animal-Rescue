About us

Compassionate Critters Animal Rescue was founded just last year in 2022 with the mission of rescuing and rehabilitating animals in need while advocating for their welfare. the organisation began as a grassroots eefort by a group of passionate volunteers dedicated to providing shelter, meidcal care and adoption services for abondoned, abused and neglected animals. Over the years, their commitment to compassion and empathy has grown, expanding their reach to neighbouring communitites, hosting adoption events and collaborating with local shelters to find loving homes for countless animals. with a strong emphasis on education and community engagement, Compassionate Critters Animal Rescue continues to make a meaningful difference in the lives of animals and the people who adopt them

Oscar

Oscar is a charming brown Labrador puppy, brimming with boundless energy and an ever-wagging tail. With his soft, chocolate-colored fur and soulful brown eyes, he's an absolute heart-stealer. He's incredibly friendly, adores belly rubs, and thrives on playful interactions with both humans and other furry friends. Oscar is a quick learner, eagerly mastering new tricks with his intelligence and eagerness to please. His zest for life and affectionate nature make him an ideal companion for anyone seeking a loyal and loving furry friend.

Despite its small size, the Shih Tzu carries itself with a dignified air, blending a playful spirit with an innate gracefulness. Whether prancing around or nestled in a cozy corner, this white Shih Tzu puppy brings boundless joy and warmth to any home, making every day brighter with its presence.

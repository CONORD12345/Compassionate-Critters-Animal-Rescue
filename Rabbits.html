Bugs

Bugs is a delightful small rabbit whose fluffy light brown fur is as soft as a cloud. Despite being small in size, Bugs has a personality that shines brightly. Their expressive twitching nose and curious whiskers show just how inquisitive and playful they are. Bugs loves hopping around, exploring their surroundings, and discovering new nooks to hide in. With big, bright eyes that sparkle with mischief, Bugs is both mischievous and endearing. This little rabbit enjoys gentle cuddles and nibbling on fresh veggies, making them an adorable addition to any home seeking a cuddly and charming companion.
